About EnseñaPerú:

We are a civil association without profit, with a mission to decisively address the main issue of Peru: education. To that end, EnseñaPerú recruits talented Young Leaders from top universities and institutes in the country who then test their talent and profession exercising leadership in schools in vulnerable areas for 2 years of teaching, training and managing. Their mission is to work full time for 2 years at schools in vulnerable areas of Arequipa, Lima and Callao.

The project currently has 67 EnseñaPerú Young Leaders, committed professionals this year will impact the lives of more than 5,300 children and young people in vulnerable areas of Arequipa, Lima and Callao. By 2014, this movement is expected to be present in at least 7 regions, which is expected to impact the lives of 35,000 children and young people and transform their environment and community, giving them the opportunity to enjoy a quality of life through a different excellent education.

EnseñaPerú a member of the global network for education Teach For All (www.teachforall.org), the model is present in over 19 countries around the world, where thousands of young people and began to work to turn around educational inequity in places as diverse as Germany, Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Chile, China, Estonia, India, Israel, Latvia, Lebanon, U.S., Spain, United Kingdom, but with only one challenge: education.

In addition, we have the backing of major organizations involved with education such as the Interbank Group, DHL, the Instituto Apoyo, JP Morgan, IBM, among others, in addition to the support of a committed Board consisting of renowned Peruvian leaders like Chef Acurio, former Finance Minister Fernando Zavala, Vania Masías professional dancer, ex - CEO of Procter & Gamble Peru Jaime Raygada, Grupo Apoyo’s president Felipe Ortiz de Zevallos, among others.

Today, this movement in education has two generations of young leaders graduated from the best universities and institutes in the country, 67 professionals representing 12 different regions, which means half the country, leading schools in vulnerable areas in the regions of Lima , Callao, Arequipa and now, starting a unique experience in Peru.
